Ikatan Remaja Islam

Kami adalah ekstrakurikuler keagamaan SMAN 1 Bandung, IKARIS yaitu Ikatan Remaja Islam. Di sini, kami berusaha menciptakan lingkungan yang mendukung remaja dalam memperkuat iman, mengembangkan wawasan keislaman, dan membangun persaudaraan yang kokoh. Di sini kami hadir dengan kegiatan menarik seperti tahfidz, mentoring, beres-beres masjid, futsal, kegiatan sosial di bulan ramadhan dan lain-lain. Temukan berbagai informasi mengenai program, kegiatan, dan acara yang kami selenggarakan untuk membantu remaja menjadi generasi yang unggul dan berakhlak mulia. Yuk jadi bagian dari keluarga IKARIS!

*Translate

We are the religious extracurricular activity at SMAN 1 Bandung, known as IKARIS (Ikatan Remaja Islam), or Islamic Youth Association. Here, we aim to create an environment that supports teenagers in strengthening their faith, developing Islamic insights, and building strong brotherhood/sisterhood bonds. We offer engaging activities such as Quran memorization (tahfidz), mentoring, mosque cleaning, futsal, social activities during Ramadan, and more. Discover various information about our programs, activities, and events designed to help teenagers become outstanding individuals with noble character. Come and join the IKARIS family!
